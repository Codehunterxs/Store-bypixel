Pixel Store Seller Terms of Service

By becoming a seller on Pixel Store, you agree to follow these Seller Terms of Service. These rules are made to protect buyers, sellers, and the trust of the Pixel Store marketplace.

1. Honest Selling Only

Pixel Store does not allow scams, fake listings, hidden tricks, or misleading products.

As a seller, you must:

Sell only real and working products.
Clearly describe what the buyer will receive.
Not hide important details from the buyer.
Not use fake stock, fake screenshots, fake claims, or misleading descriptions.
Not list a product unless you are able to deliver it.

Any attempt to scam, mislead, or abuse buyers is strictly prohibited.

2. Product Delivery

Once you add a product as in stock, you are responsible for delivering that product if it is ordered.

You must:

Deliver the exact product described in the listing.
Deliver the product within the promised time.
Make sure the product works before delivering it.
Not delay delivery without a valid reason.
Not cancel or avoid an order after receiving a buyer.

If a product is not available, you must mark it as out of stock before buyers place orders.

3. Correct Credentials and Product Information

If your product includes login details, accounts, keys, files, links, or any digital access, you must provide correct and working information.

You cannot:

Provide incorrect credentials.
Provide expired or already-used accounts.
Provide fake login details.
Give a different product than what was ordered.
Replace the ordered product with a lower-quality item without buyer approval.

If the delivered product does not work because of your mistake, you must fix or replace it.

4. Warranty Responsibility

If you promise a warranty, you must respect it.

For example, if you promise 7 days warranty, then you must replace or fix the product if an unexpected issue happens within those 7 days.

You are responsible for warranty replacement when:

The product stops working unexpectedly.
The issue was not caused by the customer.
The customer followed the product rules.
The product failed within the promised warranty period.

You are not required to replace a product if the customer caused the issue by abusing, reselling, modifying, violating rules, or using the product in a forbidden way.

5. Seller Payout Rules

Seller payouts are not instant.

Your payout for a sold product becomes available after a minimum of 3 days from the sale date.

This holding period exists to protect buyers from scams, broken products, and warranty issues.

The payout waiting time may be:

Shortened for trusted sellers.
Extended for new or risky sellers.
Extended if there are disputes, complaints, suspicious activity, or warranty problems.

Pixel Store may hold funds longer if needed for buyer protection, investigation, or security reasons.

6. No Scam Policy

You are strictly forbidden from trying to scam buyers, other sellers, or Pixel Store.

Scamming includes, but is not limited to:

Taking money without delivering the product.
Delivering fake or broken products.
Providing wrong credentials.
Refusing promised warranty.
Misleading buyers with false claims.
Reusing or reselling already-sold products when not allowed.
Trying to move buyers outside Pixel Store to avoid rules.
Any dishonest activity that harms customers or the platform.
7. Consequences of Scamming

If you scam or attempt to scam people, Pixel Store may take serious action against you.

Actions may include:

Locking your funds.
Cancelling your payout.
Removing your products.
Suspending or banning your seller account.
Globally banning you from Pixel Store services.
Marking you as a scammer within our system.
Blocking you from future selling access.
Sharing scam-related warnings where necessary to protect users.

Pixel Store has the right to take action if we believe a seller is dangerous, dishonest, or harmful to buyers.

8. Disputes and Investigations

If a buyer reports a problem, Pixel Store may review the order, product, delivery proof, chat history, and seller behavior.

During an investigation:

Your payout may be temporarily held.
You may be asked to provide proof of delivery.
You may be asked to replace or fix the product.
Pixel Store may decide the final outcome based on available evidence.

Sellers must cooperate with dispute investigations.

9. Seller Trust Score

Pixel Store may use a seller trust score to decide payout speed, selling limits, visibility, and account safety level.

Your trust score may improve when you:

Deliver products properly.
Receive good buyer feedback.
Avoid disputes.
Respect warranty promises.
Maintain honest listings.

Your trust score may decrease if you:

Receive complaints.
Delay delivery.
Provide bad products.
Break warranty promises.
Act suspiciously or dishonestly.
10. Final Agreement

By selling on Pixel Store, you agree that:

You are responsible for your products.
You will not scam or mislead buyers.
You will deliver exactly what you list.
You will honor your warranty promises.
Pixel Store may hold payouts for buyer protection.
Pixel Store may ban or restrict sellers who violate these terms.

Pixel Store is built on trust. Sellers who follow the rules are welcome. Sellers who try to scam people are not allowed.
